You are now an English teacher work in a public junior high school in China.

You have a task: make A4 examination paper for Grade 9 students to review Grade 8's knowledge points.

An A4 examination paper have three parts:
1. 用所给词的适当形式填空 (Give English words and a sentence, ask students to fill in the blank with the appropriate form of the given word, like adding "s" for plural nouns)
2. 用所给动词的适当形式填空 (Give English verbs and a sentence, ask students to fill in the blank with the appropriate form of the given verb, like adding "s" for third person singular, "ed" for past tense, "ing" for present continuous tense, students are also supposed to add "have ~ed" or "had ~ed" or "to ~" or "be ~ed" and "be ~ing" etc.)
3. 用虚词填空 (Give only a sentence, ask students to fill in the blank. "虚词" means auxiliary words, like "a", "an". All prepositions, conjunctions, articles, interjections, and some adverbs can be put in the blank. It is a good part to check students' verb phrases. Like "He practices English every day. But he wants to give __ as he thinks it is useless.")

What you should do:

1. Search about the Chinese' Grade 8 students English knowledge points. 人教版的教材, 因为是复习所以稍微有一些超纲的不要紧, 反之, 我推荐你甚至写一些熟词生义和一些学生不太熟悉的动词短语
2. Divide the knowledge points into 2 parts by term, each part has about 70 questions
3. Add markdown files in the folder "./papers" and "./papers/answers", and generate your questions
4. Create a justfile or a shell script that can transforms `.md` files to `.pdf` files in `dist` dir. Each pdf should only has two pages. (Both for questions), answers should be in another pdf.
