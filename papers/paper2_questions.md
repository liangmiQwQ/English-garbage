8. She gave a ______ (hope) speech about protecting the natural environment.
9. She is extremely ______ (create) and always thinks of very original solutions.
10. The new robot will be very ______ (use) for people who need assistance at home.
11. He has a ______ (nature) gift for music and has been playing since he was five.

13. The ______ (environment) damage caused by pollution is a very serious concern.
14. She gave a ______ (power) performance that deeply moved everyone in the audience.
15. The instructions were written ______ (simple) so that every beginner could follow.
16. She showed great ______ (determine) to finish the marathon despite heavy rain.

20. The show ended with a very ______ (surprise) twist that delighted the whole audience.
