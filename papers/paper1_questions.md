<p class="section-title">第一部分：用所给词的适当形式填空（每题1分，共20分）</p>

1. He exercises ______ (regular) and has stayed in great shape for many years.
2. She has a bad ______ (head) and can't concentrate on her homework at all.
3. This is the ______ (good) film I have seen all year — truly amazing.
4. She sings ______ (beautiful) than anyone else in our school choir.
5. He went to bed very late and now feels ______ (sleep) all through the morning.
6. Eating vegetables and fruit every day makes you ______ (health) overall.
7. Her long ______ (ill) kept her away from school for nearly two weeks.
8. She works ______ (care) on every project and rarely makes any mistakes.
9. He feels ______ (good) today after a long rest and a full night of sleep.
10. She made a ______ (suggest) to improve the school lunch menu for everyone.
11. He is one of the ______ (popular) players on the school basketball team.
12. The doctor said he should eat ______ (little) sugar to protect his teeth.
13. She is ______ (talent) at drawing and has won many local art competitions.
14. He behaves ______ (different) at home than he does in front of his class.
15. She always speaks ______ (quiet) in the library to avoid disturbing others.
16. The teacher found three spelling ______ (error) in his essay this week.
17. He can run ______ (far) than any other student in his whole PE class.
18. She was the ______ (kind) host — everyone felt warmly welcomed that evening.
19. This restaurant serves the ______ (fresh) seafood in the entire city.
20. He was ______ (luck) enough to recover very quickly from his bad cold.

<p class="section-title">第二部分：用所给动词的适当形式填空（每题1分，共25分）</p>
