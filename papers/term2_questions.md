<p class="section-title">第一部分：用所给词的适当形式填空（每题1分，共20分）</p>

1. She showed great ______ (determine) during her recovery from the injury.
2. The earthquake caused widespread ______ (destroy) across the region.
3. He received good medical ______ (treat) at the city hospital.
4. She spoke with great ______ (confident) during her first job interview.
5. ______ (fortunate), the firefighters arrived before the fire could spread.
6. The ______ (pollute) of local rivers has become a serious problem.
7. He felt a deep sense of ______ (achieve) when he crossed the finish line.
8. The children were ______ (delight) by the surprise magic performance.
9. The ______ (science) published a major paper on climate change.
10. She is the most ______ (response) member of the whole team.
11. He is ______ (fame) for his generosity toward people in need.
12. The historic bridge was ______ (damage) in last year's severe flood.
13. She spoke ______ (anger) when she discovered what had really happened.
14. He was ______ (deep) moved by the old soldier's touching story.
15. The ______ (kind) of strangers helped her survive the difficult time.
16. She made an ______ (ordinary) discovery that changed modern medicine forever.
17. He failed the test because of his own ______ (care).
18. The company had a ______ (profit) year despite the economic downturn.
19. The charity provides shelter for ______ (home) people in the city.
20. Her teacher gave her ______ (value) advice before the final examination.
